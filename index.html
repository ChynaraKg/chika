LAGMAN

  • Time: 40- 50 minutes
  • Serves:Hot
Ingredients Quantity
meat 0,5 kg
onion 2 pieces
red pepper 4 pieces
celery 1 bundle
Chinese cabbage 1 small piece
garlic 8 cloves
tomato paste 3 teaspoons
vegetable oil 200 gr
noodles 1 kg

Step 1: Add oil to a hot frying pan and fry the beef until it's cooked. When the beef is cooked, add the tomato paste and fry for a few more minutes until its fully coated the beef and the tomato paste has cooked a bit.
Step 2: Finish by adding some garlic in the last few minutes of cooking.
Step 3:Add the onions to the frying pan of beef. Add the red peppers and fry them quickly together.
Step 4:Add cabbage and celery, a cup of boiling hot water. Bring mixture to a boil and cook for a few minutes. The colour of the water should have some red in it, from the tomato paste.
Step 5:Serve as soon as the Lagman noodles are ready. Enjoy!
